April 17, 2026  ·  With all my love
My dearest Jody,

In the quiet hours before the world wakes,
I find your name written in everything —
in the way the light bends through the curtains,
in the warmth that lingers long after you've gone.

You are the kind of love I never knew to ask for,
the answer to a question I had not yet learned.
Every day beside you is a page I want to read twice,
a song I never want to reach its final verse.

Twenty-four years ago, two hearts made a promise
without knowing the weight of what they held —
and yet here we stand, fuller for every season,
richer for every storm we crossed together.

You are my home, my rest, my favourite place.
I would choose you again in every life I'm given.

Forever yours, Pabloalways & without end 🌹
